Sustainable Travel

Are EVs really green?

No doubt you've heard this question before, and the related questions: Are they greener than fuel cars? How can we all get around in the greenest way?

This section goes into more detail, but here are two rules of thumb:

  1. Cars come with many issues. Transitioning from fuel cars to electric cars only tackles two of these problems: greenhouse gas emissions; and air quality.
  2. The best way to reduce transport emissions is displacing car journeys altogether. Walking, wheeling, cycling, public transport and shared transport are all greener - as is staying at home! Electrification of car travel is second on the list. For almost all drivers, EVs will have lower lifecycle greenhouse gas emissions than fuel cars.
