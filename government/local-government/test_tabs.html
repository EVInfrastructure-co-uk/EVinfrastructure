Pavement channels

The highway authority is responsible for decisions on pavement channels, although some combined authorities in England have received the Pavement Channel Grant.

See our pavement channels page for more information.

Local Electric Vehicle Infrastructure fund (LEVI)

LEVI is England's flagship programme for installing public chargers near homes without driveways.

For more information, please see the LEVI section of our page on subsidised public charging schemes.

On-Street Residential Charging Scheme (ORCS)

ORCS was a UK Government programme for installing public chargers near homes without driveways. Any local authority could apply.

For more information, please see the ORCS section of our page on subsidised public charging schemes.

Local Electric Vehicle Infrastructure fund (LEVI) pilot

Before the main LEVI programme, some authorities took part in a pilot.

For more information, please see the LEVI section of our page on subsidised public charging schemes.
