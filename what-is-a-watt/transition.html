Number of EV drivers with driveways Zapmap Home and Community EV Charging Stats 1,355,447 2025

Zapmap's variable is number of home chargers. We use this as a proxy for EV drivers with driveways. However, this will likely underestimate EV drivers with driveways because some of these do not have home chargers.

Zapmap's estimates are based on the uptake of government grants for home chargers which are no longer applicable, and their own survey data.
Proportion of EV households with driveways Zapmap Home and Community EV Charging Stats 77% 2025

Zapmap's variable is proportion of EV households with home chargers. We use this as a proxy for proportion of EV drivers with driveways. However, this will likely underestimate EV drivers with driveways because some of these do not have home chargers.
Proportion of car households without off-street parking RAC Foundation Standing Still 75% 2021 The RAC's estimate is the proportion of cars whose home-based parking is on-street. We use this as a proxy for proportion of car households with on-street parking. However, the proportion of car households with on-street parking is likely higher than the proportion of cars parked on street because second or third cars might not fit on driveways.
