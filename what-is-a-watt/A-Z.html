The A to Z of EV infrastructure

Name Category Notes
All-Party Parliamentary Group (APPG) on Electric Vehicles Lobby/think tank Website. The Renewable Energy Association is the secretariat.
Blink Chargepoint operator Website
British Vehicle Rental and Leasing Association (BVRLA) Industry body Website. Trade body for vehicle leasing and rentals.
CENEX (Centre of Excellence for Low Carbon and Fuel Cell Technologies) Industry body Website. Part of support body for government's LEVI scheme, CENEX run NEVIS (National EV Insight and Support).
Charge Gully Cross-pavement Website. Pavement channel provider acquired by Kerbo Charge in 2025.
Chargepoint operator (CPO) Chargepoint operator
ChargeSafe Public charging auxiliary, Regulations/standards Website. Reviewing safety and accessibility of public chargers.
Charge Saint Public charging auxiliary Website. App to report bay blocking.
ChargeUK Industry body Website. Trade body for chargepoint operators.
Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) Public body Website. Competition regulator. Investigated monopolies in motorway service area chargepoints in 2022-23.
Department for Transport (DfT) Public body Website. Department associated with the Office for Zero Emission Vehicles.
Department for Energy Security and Net Zero (DESNZ) Public body Website. Department associated with the Office for Zero Emission Vehicles.
Distribution Network Operator (DNO) Electricity distribution Responsible for the distribution grid, and usually responsible for new connections to the grid for chargepoints. Your local DNO depends on where you live - check here.
Electric Car Guide Journalism/media Website. Independent website on EVs and EVI.
Electric Vehicle Association (EVA) England Consumer body Website
Energy Saving Trust (EST) Industry body Website. Independent organisation which administers several subsidised EVI schemes, forms part of the support body for local authorities, and gives consumer advice.
FairCharge Lobby/think tank Website. Group campaigning for lower VAT on public charging.
Fast Charge Journalism/media Website
EV Fleet World Journalism/media Website. Online news service covering developments in EVI from a fleet perspective.
EV UK Expo (formerly London EV Show) Conference/events Website. EV conference at the Excel Centre in London.
Field Dynamics Data/statistics Website. Geospatial data analyst. Leader in data on which households have on-street parking.
Fleet News Journalism/media Website. Online news service covering developments in EVI from a fleet perspective.
Gul-e ("Oxford/ODS Gul-e") Cross-pavement Website. Pavement channel provider associated with Oxford City Council.
(His Majesty's) Treasury (HMT) Public body Website. Ministry for taxation and public spending, responsible for VAT on charging, road tax, and DfT funding.
Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) Regulations/standards Website. Responsible for wiring regulations and guidance on electrical safety.
Kerbo Charge Cross-pavement Website. Pavement channel provider backed by Dragons' Den's Deborah Meaden.
Motability Consumer body Website. Charity which runs the public-funded Motability scheme, which funds access to cars for people with disabilities.
New AutoMotive Lobby/think tank Website. Think tank with large focus on EVs. Excellent data.
NEVIS (National EV Insight and Support) Data/statistics Website. Service offered to local authorities as part of the LEVI support body. Host of tailored metrics on EVI.
Office for Zero Emission Vehicles (OZEV) Public body Website. Formerly Office for Low Emission Vehicles (OLEV). Part of Department for Transport and Department for Energy Security and Net Zero.
Office of Gas and Electricity Markets (Ofgem) Public body Website. Energy regulator for Great Britain. Responsible for domestic price cap.
PAS 1899:2022 Regulations/standards Standard for chargepoint accessibility, developed by the British Standards Group in consultation with Motability and OZEV. We have a dedicated page and the BSI Group has guidance here.
Pavecross Cross-pavement Website. Pavement channel provider.
Public Charge Point Regulations 2023 Regulations/standards Regulations on public chargepoint operators. We have a dedicated page and gov.uk has a guidance page.
Renewable Energy Association (REA) /
Recharge UK		 Industry body Website. Recharge UK is the the REA's EVI arm. The REA provides the secretariat for the All-Party Parliamentary Group on Electric Vehicles
UK Power Networks (UKPN) Electricity distribution Website. Distribution network operator for London and the South East.
Zapmap Public charging auxiliary, Data/statistics Website. Market leader in public chargepoint mapping. Contracts with OZEV for data.
