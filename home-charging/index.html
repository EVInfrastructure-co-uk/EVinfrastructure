Home charging

By all accounts, home charging is the cheapest and most convenient way to charge your car, and the vast majority of people with EVs do charge at home. This section includes details on tariffs and grants for those who can charge at home, discussion of the driveway divide separating those who can charge at home from those who can't, and pavement channels, which can help some residents cross the divide./p>
